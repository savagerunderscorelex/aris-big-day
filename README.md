# Ari's Big Day - A Visual Novel

## About

Time Spent:

Ari's Big Day is a visual novel about a young girl named Ari who has just graduated from preschool. To celebrate this big milestone, she wants to do many activities, but she doesn't know which ones to pick! Help her celebrate her big day by traveling throughout the city and picking different activities to do with Ari!

This game was made using Ren'Py!
